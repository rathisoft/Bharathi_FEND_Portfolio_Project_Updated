Cockatoo

A cockatoo is a type of parrot and is recognisable by the showy crests and curved bill. It's plumage is generally less colourful than that of other parrots, being mainly white, grey or black and often with coloured features in the crest, cheeks or tail. On average it is larger than other parrots.

Giraffe

Giraffe is the tallest living terrestrial animal. The giraffe's chief distinguishing characteristics are its extremely long neck and legs, its horn-like ossicones, and its distinctive coat patterns.
